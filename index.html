The mind behind the code.

Hello, I am Rafi Miazi

Web Developer & Designer

I'm a 20-year-old self-taught programmer from Bangladesh who's deeply passionate about crafting digital creations, spanning websites to applications. My goal is to seize opportunities that foster my growth in the tech industry. I thrive on challenging projects and enjoy collaborating with diverse personalities.

My expertise lies in JavaScript and its derivatives like React, Angular, TypeScript, and Next.js. Frontend development is my true calling, and as a full-stack developer, I take pride in blending frontend excellence with backend considerations for seamless integration. My focus is on delivering top-notch, user-friendly applications that positively impact users.
